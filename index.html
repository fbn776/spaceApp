Pocket Space

The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it, either directly or indirectly.

A star is an astronomical object consisting of a luminous spheroid of plasma held together by its own gravity

A galaxy is a gravitationally bound system of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas, dust, and dark matter

An astronomical object or celestial object is a naturally occurring physical entity, association, or structure that exists in the observable universe.

A rocket is a spacecraft, aircraft, vehicle or projectile that obtains thrust from a rocket engine.

A space probe, or simply probe, is a robotic spacecraft that doesn't orbit the Earth, but instead explores farther into outer space.
